# RVS Navigation Prototype

A interactive navigation mockup for the Royal Voluntary Service website redesign.

## Overview

This is a single-file HTML prototype demonstrating a proposed mega-menu navigation structure across three levels, reflecting the revised site map.

## Structure

- **Volunteer** — Why and How to Volunteer · Volunteering Opportunities · Volunteer Stories
- **Get Help & Support** — Support at Home · Social Activities · Dementia Support · Support in Hospital · Find a Service
- **Get Involved** — Make a Donation · Weekly Lottery · Leave a Legacy Gift · Donate in Memory · Fundraise for Us
- **Partner with Us** — Commission Services · Corporate Fundraising & Partnerships · Employee Volunteering · Trusts, Foundations & Philanthropy
- **Retail & Cafés** — Our retail and café offer · 1938 Coffee · New Opportunities
- **About** — Our Mission and History · Our People · News and Press · Research and Policy · Impact and Reporting · Careers

## Usage

Open `index.html` directly in a browser, or serve locally:

```bash
npx serve . -p 3000
```

Then visit [http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000).

- **Desktop** (>900px): click nav items to open mega menus
- **Mobile** (<900px): tap ☰ to open the slide-out drawer
